How to Play

Blast your way through crates, dodge enemies, and clear every enemy on the grid to reach the next level. Levels get tougher as you go.

Controls

2Move up
8Move down
4Move left
6Move right
5Place bomb / Fire
0Pause game
Menu (LSK)Open pause menu
Back (RSK)Close menu / go back

Tips

  • Destroy brown crates for points — some drop power-ups.
  • B = +1 bomb you can place at once.
  • F = bigger blast radius.
  • Heart = extra life.
  • Bombs explode after a couple of seconds — don't get caught in your own blast.
  • Bombs chain-react: one blast can set off another nearby.
  • You can't walk through your own bomb once you've stepped fully off its tile.
